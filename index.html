Selam Technical and Vocational College

Selam Children’s Village (SCV) is a nongovernmental, nonprofit organization established in 1986 and reregistered with the new Civil Society Organizations Proclamation 11113/2019 with registration number 1043. SCV envisions enabling orphans and destitute community children to improve their lives and holistically develop as self-sufficient citizens. Selam Technical and Vocational College is an extension of Selam Children’s Village that provides Technical and Vocational Training to children who grow up in the organization, and applicants from the communities at subsidized cost-sharing and scholarship schemes.

Heavy Duty Equipment and Commercial Vehicle Academy

Heavy Duty Equipment and Commercial Vehicle Maintenance Academy (HDECOVA) was established with the collaboration through Public Private Partnership (PPP) with United Nations Industrial Development Organization (UNIDO), Volvo, SIDA (Swedish International Development Agency) and Selam Children’s Village(SCV). The training was started with the purpose of providing highly skilled and trained heavy duty equipment and commercial vehicle maintenance technicians for the growing market need. In addition it has transformed the automotive department of SDRTVC.
